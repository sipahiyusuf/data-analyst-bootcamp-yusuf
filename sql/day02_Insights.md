Insight 1

Observation and Comments: We can see that every year total revenue of the October and December is the almost double of the previous months. For the rest we can say that overall
revenue is geting greater each year. However the imbalances between the months can cause the troubles for logistic and stocks. Also this situation can cause 
problems about the payments of the company

Recommendations: Company can use marketing strategies to get higher revenues for the rest 10 months. This will help to establish balance. Also company should be ready 
for the October and November sales in terms of stocks ang logistics.
